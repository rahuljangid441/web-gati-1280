INTRODUCTION OF PM GATISHAKTI

Infrastructure is the backbone for enabling manufacturing and logistics competitiveness, building social connectivity and driving the economic growth of a nation. Today, as India stands at the cusp of its journey towards a century of independence, infrastructure is accelerating the roadmap to our vision of becoming Viksit Bharat (developed India)

GATISHAKTI APPROACH

PM GatiShakti combines technology-enabled data driven
decision support system with a collaborative,whole of
goverment approach to integrate the journey of infrastructure
development, from planning to implementation. This section
details out the specifics of the GatiShakti approach which
enables this holistic developement.
