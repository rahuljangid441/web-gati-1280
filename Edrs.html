ELECTRONIC - DETAILED ROUTE
SURVEYS (e-DRS)

PM GatiShakti National Master Plan enabled data-driven, electronic detailed route surveys and automated reports for a game-changing, speedy and efficient approach to pipeline construction planning, thereby contributing to our nation's logistics strength.

Overall project scope

Create efficient, accurate and fast online survey reports at no cost for pipeline projects on the National Master Portal

Expected completion date:

~6-9 months

Cost outlay:

~Rs. 25,000 per km ($ 523 per mile)

BACKGROUND AND VISION FOR THE PROJECT

Detailed Route Surveys (DRS) are prerequisites for the planning and execution of complex pipeline projects that are important not just for transporting fuel and energy but also for driving the region's progress. This humongous task of surveying and coordinating with other ministries used to take 6-9 months, often leading to cost and time overruns for these crucial projects.

Leveraging the single portal on PM GatiShakti National Master Plan portal, the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas (MoPNG) developed an electronic Detailed Route Survey (e-DRS) tool to address this challenge.
It is a data-driven solution based on existing updated data layers of authorities on the PM GatiShakti National Master Plan portal and is a powerful catalyst in the nation's growth, revolutionising the pace, efficiency, and accuracy of pipeline construction.

PM GatiShakti National Master Plan, along with the e-DRS tool, has the potential to promote a modal shift to Pipelines under the National Logistic Policy, which aims to reduce the logistics cost in India to global benchmarks by 2030 and to create a data-driven decision support mechanism for the entire logistic system.

THE GATISHAKTI EDGE AND APPROACH

The DRS is an essential exercise for surveying difficult terrain for project planning. This includes collecting extensive data on topographic information, soil composition, hydrological data, geological assessments, etc., and accurate analysis of all the collected information. Before the PM GatiShakti approach, certain terrains might have seemed inaccessible and impossible to survey or traverse. In such situations, making informed decisions
becomes a challenge.Imagine making critical decisions such as whether a pipeline should pass through the mountains directly or be rerouted around them or about dense forest covers that make laying pipelines difficult. In such cases, rerouting them slightly could lead to substantial cost and effort savings. The need for proactive stakeholder engagement across State Authorities, the Revenue Department, Central Authorities, Highway authorities, the Ministry of Road, etc. also adds to the timelines and complexity.

WORKING OF THE E-DRS TOOL ON NATIONAL MASTER PLAN

The PM GatiShakti National Master Platform, through the development of the e-Detailed Route Survey tool, has transformed the landscape for this DRS exercise. Availability of authentic data from revenue and building departments of states, Ministry of Road and Transport Highway, Ministry of Railways, Ministry of Power, Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Ministry of Water Resource and State Irrigation departments on a single platform has enabled the creation of this tool. Further, PM GatiShakti Platform has resulted in huge time and cost savings for the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas. Using the e-DRS tool on the PM GatiShakti platform, the proposed alignments can be drawn or visualised in a matter of hours using
simple drag-and-drop features. As a result, the chainage along the route can be created apart from assessing the right of way on both sides of the alignment. Different reports can be generated from the portal. Currently, 46 different quick, efficient, and accurate online survey reports are being generated through the portal, covering all the parameters required for route analysis through a single click, such as - · Surface features like Government/Public, private land, reserved forest, etc. · List of crossings en-route Rail, Road, Rivers etc. · Cadastral Survey Report i.e., RoU acquisition details as per PMP Act · Details of crop and soil type along the route, etc.

THE EXPECTED IMPACT

The e-DRS tool has enabled the MoPNG to significantly reduce the time from 6-9 months to within a single day for planning pipeline projects. As 66% of crude and petroleum transportation is currently being handled by pipelines, it is envisaged that e-DRS will also lead to notable financial savings. The e-DRS will enable us to reach full saturation in the sector while aligning with global benchmarks.

It will help the MoPNG to expand the network of Natural Gas Pipeline with the implementation of the "One Nation One Gas Grid" vision and increase the share of natural gas from 6% to 15% in the energy mix by leveraging the PM GatiShakti for holistic development.
